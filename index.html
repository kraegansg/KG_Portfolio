About me:

After graduating from Indiana University with a focus in Telecommunications and Communications and Culture, I stepped into the music industry, where I worked a variety of positions for over a decade, including 6 years of project management. In this role, I collaborated with multiple front and back-end developers to create websites and other interactive online experiences for bands and other artists. It was this experience that reminded me of my time spent in web development and UIX classes in college. It sparked a new dream. To become a software and web developer, and to get back to my creative roots. To step out of the world of independent music, and into the world of coding. With the help of the software development program at Eleven Fifty Academy, I've started a new chapter, and am eager as I am ready to apply my professional problem-solving, development skills, and unique creative approach to your organization.
